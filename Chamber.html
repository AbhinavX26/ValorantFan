Chamber

Well dressed but equally well armed, Chamber is a French marksman and weapons designer who plays the role of a Sentinel, using his tech and guns to hold plant sites and deny entry to the attackers trying to get in. Chamber uses his Nanotech gadgets to slow down enemies, teleport across sites and land high damage bullets, making him a very dangerous site holder.
