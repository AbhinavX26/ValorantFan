Omen

A phantom of a memory, Omen plays the role of a Controller and haunts the players on the battlefield with his power of shadows. His power to control dark shadows allows him to smoke out areas and pathways, blind and deafen foes with paranoia, teleport to locations non-accessible to normal agents and lurk across the battlefields, looking for stray players waiting for their doom.
