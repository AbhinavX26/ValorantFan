Welcome To Valorant

Valorant is a 5v5 shooter game made for fun purposes that drive the new kids wild today. It is a very detailed shooting game powered up with abilities that make the players sit on the edges of their seats as the attackers rush in to plant the spike and get radianite while the defenders guard the plant sites to prevent the spike from being planted. As the players use various abilities to get an advantage in a gunfight, in the end, what matters most is your skills with the guns. Without them, you're nothing in this world of miracles.


Valorant's core is of course shooting, as it has a beautifully crafted system of guns that also have realistic recoil up to a large extent. However, the correct use of abilities, proper positioning, good communication and teamwork are equally important. No one can win Valorant alone. It's always a team game. So, you should never try going solo in this game - because it will always backfire, unless you're TenZ or something. So, find the right players to play with, and discover your style of playing the game. In the end, without individual skills, you'll just be a drag on your teammates.


Valorant does have a limited arsenal, but there is a variety in the types, each weapon suiting a certain type of player. There are snipers which are used by players to eliminate threats from far away, there are rifles which are used by precise players that always go for headshots, there are small firearms which are used when your main gun runs out of bullets, there's submachine guns for mid-ranged efficient battles and finally, there are machine guns which can be used to absolutely destroy enemies standing in groups by spraying a ton of bullets into them. Combining them with the timely use of abilities is the key to stand on top.


So, Will you be the Hunted? Or will you be the Hunter?

Only time will tell.
So fight, Agent. Fight for your life...
