Jett

Jett fulfills the role of a Duelist. A Korean by heritage and deadly fast, her wind powers allow her to dash into the enemy lines and take enemies out faster than the wind. Her Ultimate, a set of 5 deadly yet precise knives, allows her to break the rhythms of enemies and tear their defensive setups to shreds.
